FAQ / Help

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about CampusFix. Can't find your answer? Contact us below.

Is CampusFix free to use? ▼
Yes, CampusFix is completely free for students. Register with your college email and start submitting complaints immediately.
How do I get email notifications? ▼
Email notifications are automatic. Whenever an admin updates your complaint status, you receive a detailed email to your registered address.
What is complaint priority? ▼
Priority (High/Medium/Low) helps admins sort and address the most critical issues first. High priority issues like safety hazards are resolved before low priority ones.
Can I attach photos to my complaint? ▼
Yes! You can attach one image (PNG or JPG, up to 5MB) to each complaint. Photos help admins understand and resolve issues much faster.
Who can see my complaints? ▼
Only you and authorized admin accounts can view your complaints. Other students cannot see your submitted complaints.
How long does resolution take? ▼
It depends on the issue type and priority. High priority complaints are typically addressed within 24-48 hours. Low priority may take up to a week.
